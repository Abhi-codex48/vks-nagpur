Our Story

A Multi-Disciplinary
CA Firm Since 2014

VKS — Vijaywargi Khabiya & Saoji is a multi-disciplinary chartered accountancy firm based in Nagpur. Established in 2014, the firm has grown into a trusted single-window advisory practice serving organisations in Nagpur, Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Hyderabad, Indore and Raipur.

Our 80+ member team includes Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Advocates, IBBI-registered Insolvency Professionals and domain specialists working together to deliver integrated, business-relevant solutions.
