Founding Partner

CA Varun Vijaywargi

B.Com | CA · Indirect Taxes · 20 Years Experience

CA Varun Vijaywargi heads the Indirect Tax practice at the firm and is a fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India with 16 years standing in the profession. He is one of the reputed indirect tax consultants in Nagpur and is known for providing innovative structuring options within the legal framework to help businesses optimize tax costs.

He has assisted clients in transition to the GST regime and continues to advise on tax optimization through restructuring of business models and supply chains, cross-border structuring and tax litigation. His experience also covers UAE VAT advisory, excise, service tax, central sales tax, VAT, import duty structuring, Foreign Trade Policy matters, and departmental audits and investigations. Prior to starting the firm, Varun served with ELP and JCSS.

Areas of Practice

Goods and Services Tax Customs Foreign Trade Policy Special Economic Zone Import Duty Structuring Departmental Audits Investigations Cross-Border Structuring

Founding Partner

CA Pavan Khabiya

M.Com | CA · Audit & Direct Taxes · 21 Years Experience

Pavan heads the Tax Compliance practice at VK&S and oversees all audits and ongoing compliances. He qualified as a Chartered Accountant in 2003 and has extensive experience in handling tax matters across both direct and indirect taxation.

He specializes in consolidation of financial statements, due diligence review of accounting functions, implementation of accounting standards, development of internal control systems and special audit assignments. His experience in financial due diligence for M&A gives him strong insight into business risks, growth drivers and potential deal breakers. Pavan also oversees the firm's income tax litigation practice and represents clients before adjudicating and appellate authorities up to CIT (Appeals). Prior to starting this firm, he was part of the tax team at Krishna Murari Agrawal & Co.

Areas of Practice

Statutory Audit Internal Audit Trust Audit Tax Audit VAT Audit Income Tax GST Audit Due Diligence

Founding Partner

CA Ashish Saoji

B.Com | FCA · Banking, Incentives & Insolvency · 23 Years Experience

Ashish leads the Project Financing vertical of the firm and has extensive experience in preparation of detailed project reports and proposals for credit facilities with banks. He has completed the Diploma in Information System Audit (DISA) of ICAI and holds a post-graduation degree in commerce as well as a bachelor's degree in law.

He specializes in advisory and financial monitoring during project implementation, optimization of finance cost including ROI, bank charges and stamp duty implications, and assistance in obtaining Eligibility Certificates and yearly Industrial Promotion Subsidy claims under the Package Scheme of Incentives. He is also a qualified Insolvency Practitioner and has assisted various debt-laden companies before the NCLT.

Areas of Practice

Package Scheme of Incentives Project Finance Working Capital Finance Fund & Non-Fund Based Limits Bank Loan Rating Industrial Promotion Subsidy Insolvency & Bankruptcy Code NCLT Matters
